Homemade Spaghetti Sauce with Ground Beef

Description

A classic homemade spaghetti sauce with ground beef recipe. The sauce consists of garlic, onion, green pepper, diced tomato, tomato sauce, tomato paste, ground beef, and just a few spices to spice things up! The spaghetti noodles can be homemade or bought from a store, it doesn't matter that much.

This meal can be made in a total of 1 hr 15 mins (prep time = 10 mins ; cook time = 1 hr 15 mins). It will yield 8 servings.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti Noodles
  • Ground Beef
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Green Pepper
  • Diced Tomato
  • Tomato Paste
  • Oregano
  • Basil
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Cook ground beef in saucepan over medium-high heat with garlic, onion, and green pepper
  2. Stir until the meat is brown and crumbly and vegatables are tender
  3. Add diced tomatoes, tomato sauce, and tomato paste into the beef mixture and stir
  4. Add oregano, basil, salt, and pepper
  5. Simmer until thick and aromatic
  6. Serve over spaghetti noodles
