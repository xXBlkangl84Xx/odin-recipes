Creamy Pesto Chicken and Bow Ties

Description

A simple pesto chicken and bow tie noodles recipe that uses condensed cream of chicken soup.

Cook time is only 20 mins, so it's a fast and easy (but delicious) meal! It yields 4 servings.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups uncooked bow tie (farfalle) pasta
  • 2 tablespoons butter or margarine
  • 1 pound skinless, boneless chicken breast, cubed
  • 1 (10.75 ounce) can Campbell's® Condensed Cream of Chicken Soup (Regular or 98% Fat Free)
  • ½ cup pesto sauce
  • ½ cup milk

Steps

  1. Cook pasta according to package directions. Drain.
  2. Meanwhile, heat butter in skillet. Add chicken and cook until browned, stirring often.
  3. Add soup, pesto sauce and milk. Bring to a boil. Cook over low heat 5 minutes or until done. Stir in drained pasta and heat through.
